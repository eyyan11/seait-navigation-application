The Problem We Solve

Navigating the SEAIT campus can be challenging for students, faculty, and visitors due to:

  • No detailed maps of classrooms and offices
  • No available exact location information
  • No updates about classroom and office changes
  • Time-consuming manual searches
  • Dependence on asking guards, teachers, or fellow students

This leads to confusion, wasted time, and frustration, especially for new students and visitors.

Campus Navigation Challenge Illustration

Our Solution

The SEAIT Navigation Application provides an interactive digital map with hard-coded, precise locations for all classrooms, offices, and facilities. It enhances the campus experience by offering detailed building layouts, visual guides, and centralized information.

Business Model

Revenue Streams

  • In-App Advertisements: Non-intrusive ads from local businesses like coffee shops and bookstores.
  • Premium Features: Tiered model with advanced features like AR wayfinding and event-specific navigation.
  • Licensing: Licensing the application framework to other educational institutions.

Competitive Advantage

The SEAIT Navigation Application holds a decisive competitive edge over general-purpose platforms like Google Maps by delivering hyper-localized, campus-specific functionality.

While Google Maps excels at street-level navigation, our application offers precise, room-level guidance inside campus buildings.

Technology Stack

  • Flutter: For cross-platform mobile application development
  • Dart: Primary programming language for application logic
  • Offline Maps: Hard-coded campus data for offline functionality
